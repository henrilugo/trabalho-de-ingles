Greta Thunberg

A Young Leader for the Climate

Profile

Greta Thunberg is a Swedish environmental activist.
She is determined, brave, and inspires millions of people.
She has become a global symbol in the fight against climate change.

About Her

• Born in Stockholm, Sweden, in 2003.
• Diagnosed with Asperger’s syndrome.
• Known for her commitment to environmental protection.
• Has become one of the most powerful young voices in the world.

Life Experiences

1. Greta has spoken at many international conferences.
2. She has inspired millions to join climate strikes.
3. Greta has met with world leaders.
4. She has written a book with her family.

Why is Greta Important?

She represents the voice of a new generation fighting for the planet.
Her actions remind us that no one is too small to make a difference.
